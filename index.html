ULTIMATE TIC TAC TOE

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is a game played on a 3x3 grid of smaller Tic-Tac-Toe boards. Two players take turns, with one as "X" and the other as "O." Here are the key rules:

  • On the first move, any player can start in any of the nine small boards.
  • After that, players must make their moves in the small board corresponding to the opponent's last move.
  • To win a small board, a player must get three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.
  • The goal is to win three small boards in a row, column, or diagonal to win the game.
  • If a small board is already won or full, the opponent can choose any available small board for their move.
  • The game ends when one player wins three small boards in line or when all small boards are full.
